Research Interests

My research focuses on the development and application of low-rank tensor decomposition methods for accelerating electronic structure calculations. Specific areas include:

  • GW and Many-Body Perturbation Theory: Developing efficient implementations of the GW approximation using tensor compression techniques
  • Tensor Hypercontraction (THC): Designing novel dual-grid schemes for decomposing the two-electron repulsion integrals with formal O(N) scaling
  • Energy Decomposition Analysis (EDA): Constructing chemically intuitive real-space EDA schemes based on density matrix formulations
  • Block Tensor Decomposition: Exploiting spatial locality to achieve linear-scaling decomposition of large molecular systems
  • Large-Scale Quantum Chemistry: Applying these methods to molecular systems of unprecedented size at correlated levels of theory
