Welcome

I am Yueyang Zhang (张越洋), a PhD candidate in theoretical chemistry at Xiamen University (XMU) since 2022. I received my B.S. in Chemistry from Xiamen University (2018–2022). My research focuses on developing low-rank tensor decomposition methods for accelerating large-scale electronic structure calculations.

Research Highlights

  • Formally O(N³)-scaling GW calculations via block tensor decomposition for systems of unprecedented size
  • Dual-grid tensor hypercontraction (THC) enabling linear-scaling molecular integral decomposition
  • Energy decomposition analysis (EDA) in real-space density matrix formulation for chemically interpretable interaction analysis

This site hosts my publications, projects, and research interests. See the Publications and Projects pages for more details, or visit GitHub/qw-nb for code repositories.
